Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 26, 2026

MarketReady Tours ("we," "our," or "us") operates the MarketReady Tours mobile application and website at marketreadytours.com. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us when using our services:

Account Information: Name, email address, phone number, and brokerage name when you create an account, sign up for a tour, or submit a listing request.

Tour Data: Property ratings, favorites, and notes you submit during home tours.

Payment Information: Sponsorship payments are processed securely through Stripe. We do not store your credit card information on our servers.

Photos: Property photos uploaded when submitting listing requests.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

• Operate and maintain the MarketReady Tours platform
• Send you tour confirmations, reminders, and route information
• Process sponsorship payments
• Communicate with you about tours, listings, and sponsorship opportunities
• Provide tour ratings and feedback to listing agents
• Improve our services

Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information in the following circumstances:

• Tour Participants: Your name and listing information may be shared with other tour participants as part of the tour experience.
• Service Providers: We use third-party services including Firebase (data storage and authentication), Stripe (payment processing), Formspree (email delivery), and Google Maps (address lookup and routing).
• Legal Requirements: We may disclose information if required by law or to protect our rights.

Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely using Google Firebase with authentication-protected access. We use industry-standard security measures including encrypted connections (HTTPS), Firebase Authentication, and restricted API keys to protect your information.
